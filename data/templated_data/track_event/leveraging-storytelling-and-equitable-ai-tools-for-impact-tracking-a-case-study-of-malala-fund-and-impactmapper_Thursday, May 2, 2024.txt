Speakers: Alexandra Pittman
Event Title: Leveraging storytelling and equitable AI tools for impact tracking: A case study of Malala Fund and ImpactMapper
Event Date: Thursday, May 2, 2024
Event Start Time: 11:30 AM
Event End Time: 12:15 PM
Event Description: This session will demonstrate the work that Malala Fund and ImpactMapper are doing together leveraging storytelling and technology in building an effective monitoring, evaluation and learning system for the foundation. Malala Fund will share a few powerful examples of case studies from their grant report data that demonstrate how to use storytelling and the ImpactMapper platform for MEL.
