Speakers: Isana Nambiar
Event Title: Emerging Technologies for Climate Change Mitigation and Adaptation
Event Date: Thursday, May 2, 2024
Event Start Time: 8:00 AM
Event End Time: 8:45 AM
Event Description: In this session we will discuss three real-world case studies from India on how AI/ML, Remote Sensing and Web3 technologies are being harnessed for climate change mitigation and adaptation. The case studies will broadly cover recent advances in the following application areas: a. Early-stage detection of devastating crop diseases to prevent yield destruction b. Remote mapping, monitoring and trend analysis of soil health and agroforestry data on smallholder farms c.
