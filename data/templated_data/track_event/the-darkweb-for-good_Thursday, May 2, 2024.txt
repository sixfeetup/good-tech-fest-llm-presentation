Speakers: Mike Dixon
Event Title: The Darkweb for Good
Event Date: Thursday, May 2, 2024
Event Start Time: 8:00 AM
Event End Time: 8:45 AM
Event Description: In 2024, Tor and other darknet technologies are well known but still remain a mysterious phenomenon often associated with nefarious activities and cybercrime. This session explores examples of how organizations are leveraging the dark web for good, from ensuring privacy rights of citizens to countering human trafficking.
